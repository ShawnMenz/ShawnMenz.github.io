What have I learned from work

  1. Embrace mistakes as learning opportunities: It’s natural to feel frustrated or embarrassed when making mistakes. Mistakes can be valuable stepping stones for growth. The most important thing is to learn from your missteps to avoid repeating them in the future.
  2. Always maintain a tracking: Keep a thorough record of your tasks and progress. Take the time to track details such as server node activities, completed tasks, command line outputs, etc. By doing so, you’ll not only save time but also have a reliable reference for future analysis and troubleshooting.
  3. Think twice: Before taking action or speaking, think twice. Recognize that thoughtless behavior can lead to significant consequences.
  4. Master the art of asking questions: Ask questions wisely. It doesn’t mean never ask silly questions. Explore the question first and attemp to identify where you’re really getting stuck. Asking for inspiration is always better than ask for answers.
  5. AI is your copilot: AI can help you achieve lots of things. Be sure to make good use of it to facilitate your research and enhance your efficiency. However, don’t abuse it. Your intelligence is about creativity which cannot be replaced by artificial intelligence. Don’t rely on AI, rely on yourself.
  6. Every cloud has a silver lining: No matter how bad it is, you can always find something good. No matter how good it is, you can always find something bad.
  7. Brief is wit: Try not to explain things with terminologies. Introduce terminology until you really need to do so and explain it in human words.
